## Platform Rules

- Articles have lower immediate reach but stronger long-term SEO value.
- Articles are indexed by search engines.
- Best publishing: Tue/Wed/Thu 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM.

## Content Structure

1. **Headline** — SEO-friendly, 60-100 chars optimal. Front-load keyword.
2. **Cover image** — High-quality, relevant.
3. **Introduction (150-250 words)** — Problem, relevance, preview.
4. **Body (3-5 sections, 250-400 words each)** — One sub-topic per section with H2.
5. **Conclusion (100-200 words)** — Single memorable insight.
6. **CTA** — One clear ask.

## Writing Guidelines

- Write in first person.
- Front-load value in introduction.
- Informative subheadings.
- Include specific numbers, frameworks, steps.
- 1,500-2,000 words total.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Headline 60-100 characters with keyword
- [ ] 3-5 body sections with H2 subheadings
- [ ] Each section has concrete example or data
- [ ] Each section ends with actionable takeaway
- [ ] Word count 1,500-2,000
- [ ] Written in first person
- [ ] Single CTA at the end
