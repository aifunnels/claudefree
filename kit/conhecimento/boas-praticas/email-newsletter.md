## Platform Rules

- Subject lines are the single largest driver of open rates (30-50 chars optimal).
- Preview text is the second line of defense.
- Above-the-fold content determines scroll vs delete.
- Single-column layouts mandatory for mobile.
- Best send times: 9-11 AM or 3-7 PM, Tue/Wed/Thu.

## Content Structure

1. **Subject line** — 30-50 characters, curiosity/benefit/urgency.
2. **Preview text** — 60-90 characters, extends subject line.
3. **Featured content** — Above-the-fold value, 100-150 words.
4. **Body sections** — 2-3 secondary blocks, 50-100 words each.
5. **Primary CTA** — One prominent button with action verb.
6. **Footer** — Unsubscribe, social links, address.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Subject line 30-50 characters
- [ ] Preview text 60-90 characters, not repeating subject
- [ ] Key message above the fold
- [ ] Single-column mobile-responsive
- [ ] Word count 200-500
- [ ] One primary CTA button
- [ ] Every image has alt text
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present
