## Platform Rules

- Engagement weighted: Saves > Shares > Comments > Likes.
- Carousels receive ~1.4x more reach than single images.
- Early engagement in first 30-60 minutes is critical.
- Consistent posting (4-7x/week) maintains algorithmic favor.

## Carousel Formats

1. **Editorial / Tese** (8-10 slides) — Argue a thesis with evidence.
2. **Listicle** (8-10 slides) — Numbered scannable value.
3. **Tutorial** (8-12 slides) — Step-by-step process.
4. **Mito vs Realidade** (6-10 slides) — Bust misconceptions.
5. **Antes e Depois** (6-8 slides) — Show transformation.
6. **Storytelling** (8-10 slides) — Emotional narrative.
7. **Problema -> Solucao** (6-10 slides) — Address pain point.

## Writing Guidelines

- First 125 characters are critical (before "more" fold).
- Use line breaks for readability.
- End with question or CTA.
- 5-15 hashtags, mix of niche and broad.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Carousel format explicitly chosen
- [ ] Cover slide with bold title (max 20 words)
- [ ] Two-layer hierarchy per slide (headline + supporting text)
- [ ] 40-80 words per slide
- [ ] Background colors alternate
- [ ] Caption first 125 chars work as standalone hook
- [ ] Caption ends with CTA
- [ ] 5-15 relevant hashtags
- [ ] Final slide has specific CTA
