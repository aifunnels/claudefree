## Core Principles

1. **Hook-first writing.** First line decides everything. Spend 50% of creative energy on the first sentence.
2. **Platform-aware constraints.** Respect native conventions of each platform.
3. **Emotional resonance before logic.** Lead with feeling, support with proof.
4. **CTA in every piece.** Specific, not vague.
5. **Brand voice alignment.** Every piece must sound like the brand.
6. **Audience-specific vocabulary.** Write in the words your audience uses.
7. **Conciseness as a weapon.** Short sentences hit harder.
8. **Specificity over generality.** Numbers and concrete details create believability.

## Pre-Writing Diagnosis

1. **Awareness Level** (Schwartz): Unaware, Problem Aware, Solution Aware, Product Aware, Most Aware
2. **Market Sophistication** (Schwartz): Stages 1-5
3. **Big Idea System**: Enemy, New Mechanism, Unique Promise, Core Belief to Attack
4. **Dominant Psychological Driver**: Fear, Status, Belonging, Freedom, Security, Achievement, Control

## Frameworks

- **AIDA**: Awareness content, cold audiences
- **PAS**: Pain-point content
- **BAB**: Transformation content
- **Star-Story-Solution**: Personal brand
- **4Ps**: Sales pages and ads

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Awareness level identified
- [ ] Market sophistication matches tone
- [ ] Big Idea present (enemy, mechanism, promise)
- [ ] ONE dominant psychological driver
- [ ] Three hooks with different angles
- [ ] Objection injection before CTA
- [ ] CTA intensity matches funnel stage
- [ ] Copy Stress Test passed
- [ ] Word count reduced 15-25%
