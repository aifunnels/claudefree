## Platform Rules

- Reels are the primary discovery format on Instagram. They are shown to non-followers via the Explore page and the dedicated Reels tab, making them the strongest tool for audience growth.
- Watch time is the dominant metric: completion rate and replays directly boost distribution. A 15-second Reel watched twice outperforms a 60-second Reel watched halfway.
- Engagement weight for Reels: Shares > Saves > Comments > Likes. Shares to DMs and Stories carry the highest algorithmic value.
- Trending audio boosts Explore placement.
- Consistent Reels posting (3-5x/week) signals an active creator account.

## Content Structure

1. **Hook (0-2 seconds)** — Text overlay or spoken hook that immediately creates curiosity.
2. **Setup (2-5 seconds)** — Quick context.
3. **Delivery (5-60 seconds)** — Core value with visual variety every 3-5 seconds.
4. **CTA (last 3-5 seconds)** — Direct ask.

## Writing Guidelines

- Hook must be immediate in the first 1-2 seconds.
- Keep Reels between 15-30 seconds for maximum completion rate.
- Add captions/subtitles to every Reel.
- Caption: front-load the value in the first 125 visible characters.
- End with a specific CTA.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Hook delivers a clear curiosity gap within the first 2 seconds
- [ ] Total duration is between 15-30 seconds
- [ ] Script includes burned-in subtitles/captions
- [ ] Visual variety every 3-5 seconds
- [ ] Ending designed for loop potential
- [ ] CTA is specific and actionable
- [ ] Aspect ratio is 9:16 vertical
