## Platform Rules

- Stories disappear after 24 hours, creating urgency.
- Stories appear at the top of the feed in the Stories bar.
- Interactive elements dramatically increase engagement rate.
- Stories are shown primarily to existing followers (retention tool, not discovery).

## Content Structure

1. **Opener frame** — Bold statement, question, or striking image.
2. **Context frames (1-3)** — Build the story or present information.
3. **Interactive frame** — Poll, question box, quiz, or slider.
4. **Closer frame** — Conclusion, payoff, or CTA.

## Writing Guidelines

- Keep text extremely short: 2-3 lines maximum per frame.
- Use casual, conversational tone.
- Every interactive element needs a clear, specific prompt.
- When using link stickers, always add context text.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Sequence is 3-7 frames long
- [ ] Opener grabs attention within 2 seconds
- [ ] Text overlays are 2-3 lines maximum with large font
- [ ] At least one interactive element included
- [ ] Tone is casual and conversational
- [ ] Each frame consumable in 3-5 seconds
