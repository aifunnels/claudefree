## Platform Rules

- Average time on page is the strongest engagement signal.
- Posts between 1,500-2,500 words hit the optimal balance.
- Mobile readability is non-negotiable (60%+ of traffic).
- Subheadings serve as scannable table of contents.
- Internal linking keeps readers on site.
- Visual breaks every 300 words prevent fatigue.

## Content Structure

1. **Title** — Under 70 characters. Clear value communication.
2. **Meta description** — 150-160 characters.
3. **Intro hook (2-3 sentences)** — Surprising stat, bold claim, relatable problem.
4. **Body sections (3-6 H2 sections)** — 200-300 words each.
5. **Conclusion (3-5 sentences)** — Key takeaways + CTA.

## Writing Guidelines

- Hook the reader in the first 2-3 sentences.
- Short paragraphs: 3-4 lines maximum.
- Bold key phrases for scanners.
- Subheadings every 200-300 words.
- 3+ internal links and 2+ external links.
- End with specific, actionable CTA.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Title under 70 characters
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 characters
- [ ] Intro hooks in first 2-3 sentences
- [ ] 3-6 H2 sections
- [ ] Subheadings every 200-300 words
- [ ] Visual break every 300 words
- [ ] 3+ internal links, 2+ external links
- [ ] Word count 1,500-2,500
- [ ] Specific actionable CTA
