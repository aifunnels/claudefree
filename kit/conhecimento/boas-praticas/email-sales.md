## Platform Rules

- Subject line 4-7 words, personalized.
- Plain-text for cold emails.
- Reply rate is primary metric (3-5% baseline).
- Follow-up cadence: days 3-4, 7-10, 14.
- Limit 35-40 cold emails/day per address.

## Content Structure (PAS)

1. **Subject line** — 4-7 words, specific to recipient.
2. **Opener** — Personalized reference, 1-2 sentences.
3. **Problem/Pain** — Specific pain in their language, 2-3 sentences.
4. **Solution** — Result-focused, one proof point, 2-3 sentences.
5. **Social proof** — Named company, specific metric, 1-2 sentences.
6. **CTA** — Single low-friction ask, 1 sentence.
7. **PS line** — Urgency or secondary proof.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Subject line 4-7 words, specific to recipient
- [ ] Personalized opener
- [ ] Problem in recipient's language
- [ ] Solution focuses on outcomes
- [ ] Social proof with named company/metric
- [ ] Exactly one CTA matching relationship stage
- [ ] PS line present
- [ ] Under 300 words (under 150 for cold)
- [ ] Plain text for cold outreach
