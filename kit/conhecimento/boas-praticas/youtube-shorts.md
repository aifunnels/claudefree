## Platform Rules

- Separate algorithm from long-form videos.
- Completion rate and replays are primary signals.
- Include "#Shorts" in title or description.
- Frequency: 2-3 Shorts per week.

## Content Structure

1. **Hook (0-2s)** — Instant curiosity.
2. **Delivery (2-50s)** — One point, no filler.
3. **Punchline/Loop (last 5-10s)** — Loops back to hook.

## Writing Guidelines

- Hook must be instant in first 2 seconds.
- Keep 15-45 seconds.
- Add text overlays throughout.
- One topic, one takeaway.
- Natively vertical 9:16.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Hook within first 2 seconds
- [ ] Duration 15-45 seconds
- [ ] One topic, one takeaway
- [ ] Text overlays present
- [ ] Loop design for replay
- [ ] Natively vertical 9:16
- [ ] #Shorts included
