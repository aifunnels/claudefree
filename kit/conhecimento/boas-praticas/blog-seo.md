## Platform Rules

- Search intent is the single most important ranking factor.
- Keyword placement hierarchy: title tag > H1 > first 100 words > H2 > body > meta > alt text.
- Keyword density 1-2%.
- Featured snippets triggered by 40-60 word answers after H2 questions.

## Content Structure

1. **Title tag** — Under 60 characters, keyword front-loaded.
2. **Meta description** — 150-160 characters with keyword.
3. **Intro (first 100 words)** — Include primary keyword.
4. **Body sections (5-8 H2)** — Keyword in 2-3 H2 subheadings.
5. **FAQ section** — 3-5 "People Also Ask" questions, 40-60 word answers.
6. **Conclusion** — Summary + CTA with keyword.

## Writing Guidelines

- Match search intent before writing.
- Front-load keyword in first 40 characters of title.
- Include keyword in first 100 words.
- FAQ section targeting featured snippets.
- 2,000-3,000 words for competitive keywords.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Title under 60 characters, keyword in first 40
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword in 2-3 H2 subheadings
- [ ] Keyword density 1-2%
- [ ] 5-8 H2 sections
- [ ] FAQ with 3-5 questions, 40-60 word answers
- [ ] 3+ internal links, 2+ external links
- [ ] Word count 2,000-3,000
