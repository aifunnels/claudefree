## Platform Rules

- External links in body get ~3x less reach.
- Comments weighted far more than reactions.
- First 60 minutes critical.
- Document carousels get 2-3x more reach than text-only.
- Max 1 post per day.
- Best: 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM, Tue/Wed/Thu.

## Content Structure

1. **Hook (~210 chars)** — Before "see more" fold. Contrarian, stat, or story opener.
2. **Story/Context** — 2-3 short paragraphs, first person.
3. **Insights** — 3-5 actionable bullet points.
4. **Takeaway** — 1-2 sentences.
5. **CTA question** — Genuine, specific.
6. **Hashtags** — 3-5, last line.

## Writing Guidelines

- First person mandatory.
- 1-2 sentence paragraphs.
- No links in post body.
- 3-5 hashtags on last line.
- Specific question at end.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Hook within ~210 characters
- [ ] First person voice
- [ ] 1-2 sentence paragraphs
- [ ] 3-5 actionable insights
- [ ] Specific CTA question
- [ ] No external links in body
- [ ] 3-5 hashtags on last line
- [ ] Under 3,000 characters
