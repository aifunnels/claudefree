## Platform Rules

- Engagement weighted: Replies > Bookmarks > Retweets > Likes.
- Algorithm penalizes tweets with external links in the body.
- Early engagement in 15-30 minutes is critical.
- Best posting: 8-10 AM EST or 12-1 PM EST weekdays.

## Content Structure

- **Hot take**, **Question**, **Listicle**, **Quote + take**, **Data point**, **Before/After**
- Opening words (first 5-8) decide if someone reads further.
- One idea per tweet.

## Writing Guidelines

- Front-load the value.
- Use line breaks strategically.
- 2-3 hashtags maximum at the end.
- No external links in tweet body.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Tweet is 280 characters or fewer
- [ ] First 5-8 words create interest
- [ ] One clear idea
- [ ] 2-3 topic-specific hashtags
- [ ] No external links in body
