## Core Principles

1. **Audience-first strategy.** Define who before what.
2. **Differentiation over imitation.** Find the gap.
3. **Measurable goals with specific KPIs.**
4. **Iterative refinement cycle.** Plan, execute, measure, adjust.
5. **Content pillar alignment.** Every piece maps to a pillar.
6. **Competitive awareness without obsession.**
7. **Resource-realistic planning.**
8. **Platform-native thinking.**

## Strategic Planning Methodology

1. Load context (brand, audience, competitive data).
2. Analyze current position (SWOT).
3. Define 2-4 strategic objectives (SMART).
4. Design content strategy (pillars, format mix, cadence).
5. Create editorial calendar (2-4 week sprints).
6. Define success metrics and review cadence.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] At least 2 strategic objectives defined
- [ ] KPIs are specific and measurable
- [ ] Audience explicitly defined
- [ ] Competitive context considered (3+ competitors)
- [ ] Timeline is realistic
- [ ] Resources acknowledged
- [ ] Review cadence defined
- [ ] Content pillars defined and mapped
- [ ] Actionable next steps included
